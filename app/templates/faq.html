Set & Get is an app used to stay organized and get more done. It’s a FREE bill reminder app. People use Set & Get to, set bill reminders and plan their day – minus the typical hassle & costs. Supporting a minimalist design alongside powerful features, Set & Get is suitable for any individual who would like to make the most out of their time.!

We live in a very task oriented society where success is dependent on completing tons of tasks everyday. In this overloaded world, it is easy to feel overwhelmed and bogged down by the plethora of things we need to do. That’s where Set & Get steps in – helping people take control, manage their busy lives, and feel good while doing it. With Set & Get, you can quickly set your reminders, add notifications, set customizable reminders, directly email your reminder, and sync your tasks across platforms.
